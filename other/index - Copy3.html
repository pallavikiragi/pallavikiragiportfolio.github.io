NEW MEDIA AND GENERATIVE DESIGN

These projects use computational processes to generate diverse design options, focusing on context and experimentation within new media design, which integrates audio, video, and electronic text in a global network.

MULTIMEDIA IN AUTOMOBILES

Exploring cross-modal concepts in automotive experiences unlock innovative possibilities. Through prototyping and multimedia integration, this approach transforms the user experience by merging technology with automotive design.

COVID SONIFICATION

A sonic visualization of active cases and mortality rates across India's states and union territories from the onset of COVID-19 to March 2022, created using OSC and sound synthesis. This project was featured in the Museum of Contemporary Digital Arts Summer Show 2022.

WORK EXPERIENCE

These companies and studios offered practical training that bridged my academic knowledge with real-world applications. Collaborating with industry professionals gave me valuable insights into real-world challenges and opportunities, enhancing my understanding of the field.

SOCIAL ARCHITECTURE METHODOLOGY

For this project, Chickpete, Bengaluru's bustling marketplace, was chosen as the focus. Through on-site explorations and field visits, I analyzed the interactions between human and non-human elements in gathering spaces. Key landmarks were identified as central points for rendezvous and references.

LEVI RE-BRAND STRATEGY

This project focused on brand design and strategy, reimagining Levi's as "LEVI" with a focus on sustainability. Inspired by Boro clothing, the rebrand promotes waste management and the reuse of worn-out denim, introducing a new trend in eco-conscious fashion.

PROXEMICS IN EXHIBITON SPACE

Proxemics as a method for space allocation and data collection exploring in an Exhibition and Convention centers. Plausible for pandemics, like Covid-19, how to organize a crowd-oriented space with social distancing using proximity as a factor.

PERSONAL INFOMATICS IN SMART HOMES

Lifestyle Health Apr 24, 2019

This project explored the role of personal data in smart home technology, focusing on designing products that enhance productivity and lifestyle through active data collection. It highlighted the interdependence between personalization and smart home systems.

CATERING SERVICE DESIGN

This project involved service design aimed at a specific group—elderly individuals—by mapping their perspectives and service ideas for an old age home. It also included creating a brand identity for a catering service run exclusively by seniors, highlighting their mission and purpose to promote the value and voice of the elderly.
