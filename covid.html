Covid-19 Data Sonification

  • May 2022
  • OSC, Data visualizationProcessing, Java , Excel

Sonic representation of Active cases and Mortality rate across all states and union territories in India since Covid's inception till March 2022 with the help of OSC and sound synthesis. Selected for Museum of Contemporary Digital Arts Summer Show 2022.

Data Sonicification of Active Cases and Mortality rate across all states and union territories in India since Covid's inception till March 2022. The video is Covid-19 data mapped to create visual structure and cohesive arrangements analyzing state by state with respective population arcs. The interactivity is based on using OSC (Open Sound Control) that connects devices under a local network, matching ports. By controlling OSC sliders, parameters of sound waves can be manipulated such as basic audio effects (reverb, delay), keeping the data intact. The core of this project emphasizes the experimentation with processing code and sound synthesis using simple datasets.

Selected in the Museum of Contemporary Digital Arts (MoCDA) Summer Show 2022

Outcomes

My goal was to recreate the sense of wonder with technology, facilitate engagement towards tools and manage runaway expectations. I see the airport as the interface between passengers and airlines therefore airline customers become the airport's customers. I examined the role of innovative applications of new media in visual communication by considering visual expression design with information technology (IT), flexible layout, diversified modes of transmission, and interactivity of integration. My micro-outputs include AI widgets, Flight tracker installation, multimedia kiosks, and hologram signages. These 4 co-exhibit cyberculture principles of using the internet and data as a form of technology, new creative and collaborative tools, a new social context and a medium of communication.
